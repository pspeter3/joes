Instructions

This app uses CSV data from Google Docs. To create a csv file, just choose that option in Download As. For the recruits section of the form, each line should have Name, Date, Pair where the Date field has the words either, saturday, or sunday in it and the Pair field is the pair that the individual recruit already interviewed with. Please make sure to fill in the events as well. After filling in the form, data will be generated and stored into the review section. Make sure everything there is right before asking the program to generate the schedule.
